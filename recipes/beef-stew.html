Beef Stew

A keto friendly, slow cooked beef stew

Ingredients:

  • Beef chunks
  • Onion
  • Broccoli
  • Carrot
  • Beef stock cube

Steps

  1. Chop onion and add to slow cooker
  2. Chop vegetables and add to slow cooker
  3. Add beef chunks to slow cooker
  4. Crumble in beef stock cube
  5. Turn on slow cooker in the morning and enjoy when you get home after work!
