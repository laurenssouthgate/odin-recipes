Lentil Dal

A classic Indian dish!

Ingredients:

  • Red lentils (1 cup)
  • Onion
  • Chick peas (1 can)
  • Sweet potato (1 small)
  • Coconut milk (1 can)
  • Turmeric (1 tsp)
  • Garam Masala (1 tsp)
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Fresh Corriander
  • Olive oil (1 Tbspn)

Steps

  1. Chop onion and cook until golden brow in a saucepan with the oil
  2. Add Turmeric and stir
  3. Pour in the lentils and stir
  4. Straight away add 1 cup of water, and the can of Coconut milk
  5. Bring to a simmer, and then add the Chick peas and Sweet Potato
  6. Cook until the sweet potatos are soft (25 mins or so)
  7. Chop Corriander and add to the dish as a garnish!
